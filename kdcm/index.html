About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring God's love to the community in working with orphans, needy and vulnerable to promote human transformation.We believe that everyone deserves an equal opportunity needed to help individuals and families achieve self-sufficiency.

What we do

Our Vision

Our vision is to see that orphans and other vulnerable people have opportunity to live normal life.By providing education, healthcare, and basic needs, we empower them to break the cycle of poverty through building sustainable communities.
